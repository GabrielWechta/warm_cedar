About Me

Birth

I was born on Halloween eve in 1999. After that event I am curiously happy when spooky staff happen. Furthermore, as everybody born around that time in Poland, I am used to getting presents around first of November, in Poland first of November is holiday, during which everybody gathers to go visit family graves.
So graveyards are also my happy places.
Despite that my other happy places are Hyrule, Lordran, Arkham, Northern Kingdoms and Twin Peaks.

Games

I know it may be hard to believe, especially because I am an IT engineer, but I love video games. And by love I mean absolute fascination and deep trust. It really amazes me how they are written, deigned and programmed. It is important to notice that video games are subset of games, overall. I really like games like chess and role playing games - old, board kind. When I have to much time in my bath I think about how really impressing and how absurd is idea of humankind spending time on playing. I am always amazed how "bread and circuses" turned into "bread and games", homo ludens I suppose.

Visual Art

My greatest internet discovery where youtube videos about movies, some may call them - video essays. Since I have seen video about Quentin Tarantino's techniques to enhance story by camera movement, I accidentally found my biggest fascination. Slippery slope after that, when movie is getting "meta" I am getting "in love".
Next great moment I can point in my movie experience is watching Synecdoche, New York. Wow.

Also David Lynch is my hero.
